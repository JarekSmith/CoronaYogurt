Five Nights at Freddy's 2

12/13/2025

Just watched the FNAF2 movie!

I'm just going to preface, it's not a very good movie. If you're looking to hoot and holler at "peak cinema," the FNAF2 movie is probably not going to be what you're looking for. However, it is pretty cool. I'd kind of compare it to those old monster versus movies--it doesn't have a groundbreaking story or anything that's going to make you FEEL a certain way, but it's pretty darn fun to watch these robots do stuff and be dangerous.

The fact that they're all practical (as far as I know) makes for a fun watch. They have a presence wherever they go. Watching a movie where the characters are talking to what's clearly CGI always takes me out of it, I can't just not imagine them talking to an empty room.

It's packed full of references that's sure to make your 12-year-old son/daughter chortle with glee. Definitely consider taking your child out to see this film if you need some brownie points with them, they'll love you for it.

Overall, I'd give the Five Nights at Freddy's 2 movie a solid 2.5/5 stars. Not a terrible movie, pretty enjoyable as far as video game movies go, but nothing to write home about.
